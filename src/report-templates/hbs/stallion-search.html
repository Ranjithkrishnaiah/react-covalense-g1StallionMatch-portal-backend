Perfect Match!

A Perfect Match occurs when there are 2 or more Graded Stakes Winners with common ancestors in every quarter of the pedigree of your mating. Of all Stallion Match searches, only 3 in 100 are a Perfect Match.

20/20 Match!

A 20/20 Match occurs when a mating has two or more Graded Stakes Winners with more than 20 shared ancestors within 5 generations. This is called a SI (Simalarity Index) and indicates that the mating is proven. This occurs in only 45% of all searches.
